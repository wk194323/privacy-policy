## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our autonomous agent-based application (“Application”). We are committed to ensuring that all data is handled securely, transparently, and in compliance with applicable privacy standards.

## 2. Scope of the Application

The Application:

* Operates within a controlled sandbox environment
* Uses AI agents to generate content and perform tasks
* Requires **explicit human approval before any content is published externally (e.g., LinkedIn)**
* Integrates with third-party services such as OpenAI and LinkedIn APIs

## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

* Generate and refine AI outputs
* Execute approved tasks

* Ensure compliance with security and approval workflows

## 5. Human-in-the-Loop Control

A core design principle of this Application is **human oversight**:

* No content is published externally without **explicit user approval**
* Agents operate under strict orchestration and permission controls
* Sensitive or impactful actions require validation before execution
